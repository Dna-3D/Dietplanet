About Diet Planet

Empowering FUTO Students Through Healthy & Affordable Nutrition

Our Story

At Diet Planet, we understand the challenges that come with being a student at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO). Long study hours, tight budgets, and the constant need for convenient, nutritious meals are just some of the daily realities our fellow students face.

Founded by FUTO alumni who experienced these challenges firsthand, Diet Planet was born from a simple yet powerful idea: every student deserves access to healthy, affordable food without compromising on nutrition or convenience.

We noticed that most food options around campus were either expensive, unhealthy, or both. Students were forced to choose between eating well and staying within budget. That's when we decided to create Diet Planet - a solution that bridges this gap.

Our Mission

To provide FUTO students with convenient access to nutritious, affordable, and delicious meals that support their academic journey and overall well-being.

We believe that good nutrition should not be a luxury but a fundamental right for every student striving to achieve their educational goals.

Our Vision

To become the leading food delivery service for students across Nigerian universities, setting the standard for quality, affordability, and convenience.

We envision a future where every student has easy access to healthy food options that fuel their ambitions and dreams.
