About Diet Planet

Empowering FUTO Students Through Healthy & Affordable Nutrition

At Diet Planet, we understand the challenges that come with being a student at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO). Long study hours, tight budgets, and the constant need for convenient, nutritious meals are just some of the daily realities our fellow students face.

Founded by FUTO alumni who experienced these challenges firsthand, Diet Planet was born from a simple yet powerful idea: every student deserves access to healthy, affordable food without compromising on nutrition or convenience.
